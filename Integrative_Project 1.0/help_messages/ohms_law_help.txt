	This animation shows the effect of the resistance and the voltage on the current
	of an electric circuit.

	Adjust the value of the resistance and the voltage with the sliders, 
	their values are under their respective sliders.
	The value of the current is under under the circuit.

	The dots represent the electrons in the circuit. 
	The amount of dots represents the value of the current.
 

	Hit the Start button to start the animation.
	Hit the Pause button to pause the animation.
	Hit the Continue button to continue the animation after pausing.
	Hit the Reset button to reset the animation after inputting new data.
	Hit the Back button to go back to the main menu.
